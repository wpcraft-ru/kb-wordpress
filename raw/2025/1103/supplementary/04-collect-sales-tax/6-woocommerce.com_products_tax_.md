## Frequently asked questions

 What about support and updates? 

Our automated tax offering includes a connection to WordPress.com cloud services. Just install and go!Our[documentation](https://woocommerce.com/document/woocommerce-shipping-and-tax/woocommerce-tax/)covers what you need to know. If you have additional questions, contact us.

 How much does this service cost? 

Actually, you just save. WooCommerce Tax is a free service. If you are selling from more than one store location though, you will need to use a standalone[tax extension](https://woocommerce.com/product-category/woocommerce-extensions/operations/tax/)of your choice.

 What if I’m not in an eligible country? 

Yep, we hear you. We’re working on adding more tax providers, and services so that more stores can get the benefits of WooCommerce cloud-powered services.
