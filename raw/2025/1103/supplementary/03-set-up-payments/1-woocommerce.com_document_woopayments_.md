# WooPayments Documentation

Welcome to the WooPayments documentation! Here you can find all sorts of helpful information about running your business website and taking payments. Please see the sections below for more information about what WooPayments can do.

## [Startup Guide](https://woocommerce.com/document/woopayments/#startup-guide)

[↑ Back to top](https://woocommerce.com/document/woopayments/#doc-title "Back to top")
The **[Startup Guide](https://woocommerce.com/document/woopayments/startup-guide/)** is a step-by-step walkthrough of the WooPayments basics, such as how to install this extension, the signup process, and available settings. If you’re just starting out with WooPayments, this beginner-friendly guide is perfect for you!

## [Learn more](https://woocommerce.com/document/woopayments/#learn-more)

[↑ Back to top](https://woocommerce.com/document/woopayments/#doc-title "Back to top")
Once WooPayments is up and running, our [Managing Money guide](https://woocommerce.com/document/payments/managing-money/) explains how money can flow into and out of your site. This includes topics such as listing transactions, viewing your payouts, [issuing refunds to customers](https://woocommerce.com/document/woopayments/managing-money/#refunds), and so on.

Our [Settings Guide](https://woocommerce.com/document/payments/settings-guide/) is another good document to review once you’re familiar with the basics. It explains every option included in WooPayments and how you can configure those to best suit your website, your customers, and your business model.
