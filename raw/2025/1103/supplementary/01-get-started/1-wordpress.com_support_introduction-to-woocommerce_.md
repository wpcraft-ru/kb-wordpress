 Last reviewed on April 6, 2026

To create an online store, install the free [WooCommerce plugin](https://wordpress.com/plugins/woocommerce/) on your WordPress.com site. This guide will introduce WooCommerce and help you start creating products and setting up a store.

This feature is available on sites with the WordPress.com [Personal](https://wordpress.com/support/plan-features/personal-plan/), [Premium](https://wordpress.com/support/plan-features/premium-plan/),[Business](https://wordpress.com/support/plan-features/business-plan/), and [Commerce](https://wordpress.com/support/plan-features/ecommerce-plan/) plans. ## About WooCommerce

[](https://wordpress.com/support/introduction-to-woocommerce/#about-woocommerce)

[WooCommerce](https://woocommerce.com/) is an e-commerce solution for WordPress that allows you to add a cart and checkout process, list your products, take payments, monitor inventory, and do everything else you need to sell online. You can set up an online store to sell physical products, digital products, subscriptions, memberships, services, and more.

You can install the [free WooCommerce plugin](https://wordpress.com/plugins/woocommerce) on the WordPress.com Business plan. Alternatively, choose our [Commerce plan](https://wordpress.com/ecommerce/) to expand upon the WooCommerce plugin with a suite of handpicked WooCommerce extensions and tools to help you sell products, accept payments, ship products, and grow your store.

Check out some of the WooCommerce functionalities in [this live demo](https://themes.woocommerce.com/storefront/).

## Install WooCommerce

[](https://wordpress.com/support/introduction-to-woocommerce/#install-woocommerce)

To install WooCommerce on your website, you have two options. When you purchase the **Commerce plan**, the WooCommerce plugin and a [large collection of ecommerce extras](https://wordpress.com/support/plan-features/ecommerce-plan/) are installed automatically on your site.

On the Business plan, you can install WooCommerce at no extra cost with the following steps:

1.   Visit your site’s [dashboard](https://wordpress.com/home).
2.   Go to _Plugins → Add Plugin_ and search for “WooCommerce”.
3.   Locate the [WooCommerce by Automattic](https://wordpress.com/plugins/woocommerce/) plugin and click the **Install** button.
4.   After activation, the [Setup Wizard](https://woocommerce.com/document/woocommerce-setup-wizard/) will launch automatically to guide you through the initial steps to set up your store.

## Add products in WooCommerce

[](https://wordpress.com/support/introduction-to-woocommerce/#add-products-in-woocommerce)

With WooCommerce installed, we recommend adding your first products with the following steps:

1.   Visit your site’s [dashboard](https://wordpress.com/home).
2.   Click on the new **Products** menu option added by WooCommerce.
3.   Click the “**Add product**” button and fill in the details for your first product.

To learn more about creating a product and the different types of products you can add, visit the [WooCommerce guide to adding a product.](https://woocommerce.com/document/managing-products/add-product/)

## Adjust WooCommerce settings

[](https://wordpress.com/support/introduction-to-woocommerce/#adjust-woocommerce-settings)

Visit _WooCommerce → Settings_ to adjust your store’s settings, including:

*   **General**: Your store address, selling region, currency, and more.
*   **Products**: Units of measurement, product reviews, inventory, and more.
*   **Tax**: Define rates or enable automated taxes.
*   **Shipping**: Zones, methods, and rates for shipping your products.
*   **Payments**: Set up payment methods. [WooPayments](https://woocommerce.com/document/woopayments/) is recommended where available.

Learn more about each setting in the [Configuring WooCommerce settings](https://woocommerce.com/document/configuring-woocommerce-settings/) guide.

## Design your store

[](https://wordpress.com/support/introduction-to-woocommerce/#design-your-store)

Browse our collection of [beautifully designed themes](https://wordpress.com/themes/filter/store/) purpose-built for WooCommerce stores, or explore [even more WooCommerce themes](https://woocommerce.com/product-category/themes/) to find the perfect design.

When you install WooCommerce, [essential store pages](https://woocommerce.com/document/woocommerce-pages/) including Shop, Cart, and Checkout are created automatically.

## Going further with WooCommerce

[](https://wordpress.com/support/introduction-to-woocommerce/#getting-help-with-woo-commerce)

With WooCommerce, the possibilities are endless for building a powerful, profitable website to sell products and services. To explore more, visit the[WooCommerce documentation](https://woocommerce.com/documentation/woocommerce/) for comprehensive guides to all aspects of WooCommerce.

For support with WooCommerce on your WordPress.com site, [contact our Happiness Engineers](https://wordpress.com/support/help-support-options/).
