### Introduction
How to find, activate and customize a block theme that meets your design needs. Block themes represent the future of WordPress design — built entirely with blocks, offering incredible flexibility and customization. They let you visually edit every part of your site, from headers to footers.

### Finding, selecting and activating a block theme

**Via WordPress.org:**
- Go to WordPress.org → Extend → Themes → Block Themes
- When choosing, consider: last updated date, active installations, compatibility with latest WP version, ratings, support offered

**Via Dashboard:**
- Appearance → Themes → Add new theme
- Filter by "Block Themes" or use Feature Filter

**Key insight:** With block themes you're not locked into the initial design. You're choosing a starting point to freely customize. You could start with a default WordPress theme and mould it to your needs.

Once you find the right theme: Install → Activate.

### Customizing your theme
Go to Appearance → Site Editor. Here you can:
- Edit templates
- Create new layouts
- Adjust Global Styles

**Strategy:** Choose a theme close to what you hope to achieve — saves time and effort.
- Portfolio → portfolio-focused block theme
- Business site → themes with robust homepage layouts and patterns

From there: adjust colors/typography to match brand, modify layouts, add/remove sections. Even a minimalist default theme can be used as a base.
